Deposit & Cancellation Policy Button

On click of this button a window will pop up with the details of the deposit and cancellation policy for that room type configured. This is also being fetched from the LMS system. The deposit and the cancellation amount can also be configured in the LMS which is getting populated in the Web Booking Engine.
